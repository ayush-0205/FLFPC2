About Florida Food Policy Council

Our Mission

The Florida Food Policy Council works to advance equitable, community-driven food policies that strengthen food access, climate resilience, and justice across Florida.

IMAGE AREA – Community Forum / Public Meeting

Our Vision

A Florida where food systems are community-governed, climate-resilient, and rooted in justice and sustainability.

IMAGE AREA – Policy Workshop / Advocacy Event

Our Values

  • Community-Led Governance
  • Climate & Environmental Justice
  • Equity & Inclusion
  • Collaboration Across Sectors

History & Growth

Founded in 2016, FLFPC brings together policymakers, researchers, advocates, farmers, and community leaders to shape inclusive food systems policy statewide.
